## dimlunit property


Gets or sets units mode (not applied to angular dimensions).
Possible values are: 1 (scientific), 2 (decimal), 3 (engineering), 4 (architectural stacked), 
5 (fractional stacked), 6 (Microsoft Windows Desktop).
